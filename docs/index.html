How the Slot Engine Works

What is this?

This is a Monte Carlo Simulation Engine designed to test and balance slot machine mathematics. In the casino gaming industry, math models must be rigorously tested to ensure they hit target payouts and remain profitable for the house while being engaging for the player. This engine rapidly simulates millions of mathematical permutations to converge on theoretical probabilities.

Key Metrics

  • RTP (Return to Player): The theoretical percentage of wagered money paid back over millions of spins. Example: 95% RTP means the house keeps 5%.
  • Volatility Index: A measure of the game's variance. High Volatility = infrequent but massive wins. Low Volatility = frequent but small wins.
  • Hit Rate: The percentage of spins that result in any payout greater than zero.

Game Mechanics

  • The Grid: A standard 3x5 layout evaluated across 20 distinct paylines from left to right.
  • Free Spins: Triggered by landing 3 or more Scatter (SC) symbols. Multipliers are applied during these spins.
  • Hold & Spin: Triggered by landing 6 or more Coin (CO) symbols. The coins lock in place, granting 3 respins to collect more. Filling all 15 positions awards the Grand Jackpot!
